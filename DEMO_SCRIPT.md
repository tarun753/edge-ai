# EDGE Demo Script — 4-Minute Video

## Setup
- You = the sales rep (on camera, EDGE overlay visible on screen)
- Friend/second device = the prospect (can be off-screen voice, or a second laptop)
- Screen shows: EDGE app + a fake CRM tab (Google Sheet with prospect info)

---

## The Script

### 0:00 — Hook (30 sec, no AI yet)

YOU (to camera):
> "Every year, billions of dollars in deals are lost not because of bad products —
> but because of the wrong word at the wrong moment.
> Gong tells you what went wrong after the call. That's a post-mortem.
> EDGE tells you what to do while it's happening."

*Show EDGE UI. Click "Start Session". Screen share + mic activate.*

---

### 0:30 — The Call Begins (warm up, 30 sec)

PROSPECT: "Hey, thanks for jumping on. I've been looking at your platform."

YOU: "Absolutely, great to connect. Tell me — what's driving the search right now?"

PROSPECT: "We're growing fast and our current tool just isn't keeping up."

*No whisper yet — normal conversation. Shows EDGE is NOT spamming.*

---

### 1:00 — Trigger 1: Competitor Signal (45 sec)

PROSPECT: "We've actually been demoing a couple of other solutions this week too."

*⚡ WHISPER FIRES:*
> "Competitor eval active. Don't ask who — ask what matters most: 'What does your ideal solution look like?'"

YOU (glances at overlay, uses it):
> "That's helpful context. What does your ideal solution actually look like for your team?"

PROSPECT: *opens up about their real needs*

---

### 1:45 — Trigger 2: Budget Stall (45 sec)

PROSPECT: "This looks great honestly, but we have a bit of a budget freeze right now..."

*⚡ WHISPER FIRES (HIGH URGENCY):*
> "Budget stall — not a no. Ask: 'Is this a timing issue or a priority issue?'"

YOU (uses it):
> "I appreciate you being upfront. Is this more of a timing issue, or is it a priority question for your team?"

PROSPECT: "Honestly... it's more timing. Q2 we should have budget freed up."

*Deal is alive. Audience sees the exact moment a deal was saved.*

---

### 2:30 — Trigger 3: Buying Signal (30 sec)

PROSPECT: "How long does onboarding usually take? And does it integrate with Salesforce?"

*⚡ WHISPER FIRES:*
> "Buying signal — they're past the decision. Stop selling. Move to next steps."

YOU: "Onboarding is 2 weeks. Salesforce native. Should we get a technical call on the calendar for your team?"

PROSPECT: "Yeah, that actually makes sense."

---

### 3:00 — Show the Architecture (30 sec)

*Switch to architecture diagram slide.*

> "Here's how it works under the hood.
> Audio streams to Gemini Live API in real-time.
> Screen captures every 4 seconds — so EDGE sees your CRM, their LinkedIn, your notes.
> A strategy agent synthesizes both and fires whispers only at critical moments.
> All running on Google Cloud Run."

---

### 3:30 — Close (30 sec)

> "Sales reps don't lose deals because of bad products.
> They lose them in moments like these — moments that pass in seconds.
> EDGE catches those moments. Every time.
>
> This is EDGE. Built on Gemini Live API and Google Cloud."

*Show EDGE logo. End.*

---

## Tips for Recording
- Record in one take if possible — real feels better than polished
- Keep the EDGE overlay clearly visible when whispers fire
- Pause 1 beat before using the whisper — let the audience read it
- The prospect voice can be a friend on the phone or pre-recorded audio
- Use OBS or Loom for screen + face recording simultaneously
